What this version does:
- Saves form submissions to your Formspree dashboard.
- Keeps a local browser backup only for debugging.
- Sends these fields: name, phone, email, stage, institution, source, page, referrer, device, createdAt.
- Google Analytics is intentionally not added yet.

How to test:
1. Open the HTML page.
2. Submit one test signup.
3. Go to your Formspree dashboard.
4. Open the form named for this endpoint and check Submissions.

Important:
If Formspree asks you to verify/activate the form from your account/email, complete that once before public launch.
